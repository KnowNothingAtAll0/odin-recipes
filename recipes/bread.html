Ingredients

2 ½ cups all-purpose flour 1 cup white sugar 1 teaspoon baking powder 1 teaspoon baking soda 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon ½ teaspoon salt 3 large eggs, beaten ½ cup vegetable oil ½ cup milk 2 cups shredded carrots 1 (3.5 ounce) package flaked coconut ½ cup maraschino cherries, chopped ½ cup raisins ½ cup chopped walnuts

Directions

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan. Baking ingredients for fruit and nut bread on table. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together in a large bowl. Dry flour mixture in a large white mixing bowl. Combine eggs, oil, and milk in a separate bowl; stir into flour mixture until well blended. Whisked batter mixture in bowl before adding fruits. Stir in carrots, coconut, cherries, raisins, and walnuts. Batter with shredded carrots, raisins, and cherries mixed in. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan. Fruit and nut batter spread evenly in loaf pan. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean, about 50 to 60 minutes. Let cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes before removing from the pan to cool completely.
